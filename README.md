### 🌐 System Overview
I am a multi-disciplinary **Systems Engineer** and **3D Developer** specializing in bridging the gap between physical industrial operations and cognitive virtual systems. My expertise lies in designing and building high-fidelity **Digital Twins**, integrating **Industrial IoT (IIoT)** telemetry networks, deploying **Generative AI** workflows, and crafting immersive **3D simulations** within Unity. 
I architect responsive, cyber-physical ecosystems that enable real-time monitoring, autonomous decision-making, and immersive training interfaces for smart manufacturing and industrial automation.
* **🎯 Core Mission:** Accelerating the transition to Industry 4.0 by deploying intelligent, real-time virtual replicas of complex industrial systems.
* **🛡️ Development Principles:** Performance-first design, high-fidelity physical simulation, semantic data structures, and edge-native efficiency.
---
### 🛰️ Core Specializations
#### 🌀 Digital Twin Systems
Architecting end-to-end data pipelines that bind mechanical systems to their virtual counterparts. I specialize in real-time, bi-directional telemetry synchronization, creating accurate kinematic 3D replicas, and displaying live performance overlays for rapid diagnostic response.
* **Focus:** 3D operational dashboard overlays, time-series visualization, and predictive maintenance triggers.
#### 🧠 Generative AI Applications
Deploying large language models (LLMs) and advanced machine learning to build cognitive layers on top of industrial telemetry. I build autonomous agents and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) structures to turn raw machinery logs and documentation into actionable troubleshooting pipelines.
* **Focus:** AI-powered industrial operators, autonomous agentic workflows, and semantic search interfaces.
#### 🏭 Industrial IoT Solutions
Engineering robust edge-to-cloud telemetry architectures that handle massive industrial data flows. I configure secure networking meshes, deploy edge pre-processors, and bridge classical operational technology (OT) protocols with modern cloud environments.
* **Focus:** Protocol conversion (OPC UA, Modbus, MQTT), edge anomaly detection, and high-performance ingestion meshes.
#### 🎮 Unity & 3D Simulation Projects
Crafting photorealistic, high-performance virtual environments for system commissioning, stress testing, and immersive training. I specialize in exact kinematic modeling of industrial machinery, custom physics engines, and cross-platform VR/AR deployments.
* **Focus:** Kinematic robotics simulation, virtual factory commissioning, and immersive XR training modules.
